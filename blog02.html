NO ANSWERS

By Tricia Dennis / August 12, 2019

Krakowiaks’ two children are severely disabled, yet neither has received a conclusive diagnosis. Heath and Mariel Krakowiak, Colbie and Lleyton’s parents, have had them evaluated by countless specialists. The kids have been through the National Institutes of Health Undiagnosed Diseases Program, during which their genomes were sequenced. The only thing doctors can tell the Krakowiaks is that each of their children has an individual genetic mutation about which nothing is known. No answers or diagnoses are in sight.
