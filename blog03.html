RARE RESEARCH INTO SEIZURES AND FOOD

by Tricia Dennis / October 15, 2019

We Are RARE recently began studying food related seizure issues and what can trigger episodes. We have found two tube feed formulas that contain an ingredient known to trigger seizures in even someone without epilepsy. It is always our goal to educate about seizure control and ways to avoid interactions.
